# Jon Reiner TikTok Automation Case Study

## Philosophy
"I'm obsessed with shrinking timeframes."
- Accelerates learning
- SYSTEM: Save yourself TIME, ENERGY, MONEY, STRESS

## The 28K Win: Child of God T-Shirt
- DMed top influencers, got 20 to make content
- Commission rate: ~20%
- 2 videos went viral → $4K days

## Working Method
1. Jon tried 4 products and lost thousands
2. Applied burner method
3. Product blew up → $4K/day revenue

## Burner Method
- Create new account, search a niche (e.g., "mothers day")
- Use AI to replicate successful designs (like t-shirt designs)
- Use Kalodata to find market gaps
- Check if trending on Google Search for long-term viability

## Products That Sell
1. Has a **wow factor**
2. **Solves a problem**
3. **Easy to make content** with

## SOP Reference: Gift-Giving Problem Solvers
- Products that solve gift-giving anxiety
- Address insecurities ("AWESOME LIKE MY SON" / "AWESOME LIKE MY DAUGHTER")
- Seasonal recurring revenue: Mother's Day, Father's Day
