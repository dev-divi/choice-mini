# Market Research Tools

## Kalodata
- Market analysis tool (alternative to Fastmoss)
- Use to find product gaps in the market
- Filter by sales velocity spikes
- Track competitor counts per product

## Fastmoss
- Product research and trend analysis
- Sales velocity tracking
- Competitor analysis

## PiPiAds
- Ad spy tool for TikTok
- Scrape top-performing ads for any product
- Analyze hooks, CTAs, creative angles

## AutoDS
- Dropshipping automation platform
- Marketplace for sourcing products
- Filter: "Ships from US" + "Delivery < 7 days"
- Supplier Reliability Score tracking
- API for auto-importing products to Shopify
- AI-generated SEO titles and descriptions
