9-Stage Enneagram Operating System

CHOICE AURA — PROPRIETARY FRAMEWORK — BACKUP COPY
Based on Gurdjieff's Fourth Way / Law of Seven / Law of Three. Tyler has been working within this system for 2+ years. Applies to every project ("wheel") and wheels nest inside wheels.
1
Intention
Set the aim, total being engaged
2
Tools
Gather what's needed (teachers, ritual, finances, resources)
3
Passive / Personality
Discover you don't know what to do. Hit limitation.
⚠ Point 3 Hard — "Did I just do tools, set an intention?" Got stuck not knowing what real action to take. Good time for ritual.
4
Limitation
Overcome limitations through creative means (repression, acceptance, clarity, lethargy, choices)
5
Action
Take action (prioritize, expand, create, practice)
6
Active / Essence
Putting it all together. About to reveal publicly.
⚠ Point 6 Hard — "Did I just put everything together, about to reveal a new version of me/work?" Pulled back before going public.
7
Presentation
Present what has been created to the world. True character revealed.
8
Retention CURRENT
Collect data, results, feedback. Prepare for next cycle. Surrender.
⚠ Point 8 Hard — "Did I bring a big release to world and then fallout or rejected?" The wheel dies from rejection/overwhelm.
9
Neutral / Life
Beginning of a new cycle. Return to intention.
KEY PRINCIPLES
"Hard is a sign things are working, off octave" · Life wheels run in 7-year cycles · From point 7 to 9 it's all about surrender · Wheels feed into other wheels; failures are incomplete cycles that feed forward
LAW OF THREE
Active (affirming) · Passive (denying) · Neutral (reconciling) · Nothing can happen unless all three forces are present · "Everything, positive and negative, is working for us"
THE FORMULA
yes, no, conflict, completion · Know + Thyself · CONFLICT + PURPOSE = AFFIRMING + DENYING + CONFLICT + UNITY (COMPLETION)
